GTR:
I bought my 1000GTR in July 16th 1993 when it was exactly 2 years old with 18.000km on the odo. After that I was almost every sunday morning on the road with my brother to attend to some organized tour. riding the bike That took place till about the beginning of the 21st century. By than I was mostly on the road by myself. My first GTR meeting was in the east of the Netherlands in Rijssen in 2001. Am not sure who organized that but I saw a flyer at 'de GTR-specialist' Geert (can't remember his last name) after I needed a new mirror after an encounter with a car on one of the many dikes in Holland. Did meet most of the 'old gang' in Druten on the first unofficial GTR-dag in 2002. From 2003 to 2006 didn't ride that much, until I signed on for the GCE Rally in Sweden. Since than attended most TdT and GCE Rally's until 2010. Meanwhile in 2009 I added a 1400GTR to my stable and sadly the 1000 was left most of the time in the garage. In 2013 I married Maria and emigrated to the USA
